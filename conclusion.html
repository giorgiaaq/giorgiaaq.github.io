Conclusion

The Project Methodology Process Conclusion

The proposed improvements address crucial aspects such as depiction, layout, construction elements, the role of the commissioner, and related historical events. Currently, the knowledge graph exhibits numerous gaps in linking Frederick II with his architectural commissions. These enhancements will not only enrich the quality and depth of the available information but also contribute to a better understanding of the scope of Frederick II's works and his historical impact. This project aims not only to improve the existing knowledge graph but also to lay a solid foundation for future research and studies on Italy's architectural heritage and the figure of Frederick II.
In conclusion, the investigation into Italy's architectural heritage, with a particular focus on Frederick II, has underscored the necessity for substantial enrichment of the ArCo Knowledge Graph.
